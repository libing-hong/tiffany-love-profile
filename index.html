Q1 — The Beginning

How did your story begin?

The beginning often says something about the energy of a relationship. Choose the version that feels closest to yours.

Q2 — Your Connection

What best describes your relationship today?

Think of the emotional tone between you. Not the surface, but the atmosphere you return to again and again.

Q3 — Your Partner

How would you describe your partner?

Choose the quality that feels most unmistakable, the one that seems to define their presence.

Q4 — Your Vision

When you imagine your future together, what feels right?

This is less about planning and more about emotional direction. Which future feels naturally yours?

Q5 — Your Style

Which ring style speaks to you most?

Aesthetic instinct matters. Even in a story-led consultation, visual preference is part of the emotional language.

Q6 — Your Budget

For this demo, the answer is only used as a client insight. The final three-ring presentation remains consistent for all visitors.

Q7 — Your Defining Moment

Which moment best represents your love?

A final emotional cue. The most revealing answers are often the ones that feel the quietest.
